Knowledge Management and Data Privacy at Edinburgh Tabernacle

Welcome to the Edinburgh Tabernacle Church's Knowledge Management (KM) and Data Privacy (DP) resource hub. This site is dedicated to sharing insights and recommendations on how our church can better manage the knowledge we generate and protect the sensitive information entrusted to us. My name is Sharon, and as a member of our church community, I have conducted extensive research into how we can improve these critical areas to support our mission and operations.
